# Google Notebook Video Prompts

## Overview
This document provides comprehensive instructions and prompts for creating marketing videos using Google NotebookLM's video maker tool. The following sections outline various types of videos and best practices to ensure high-quality production.

## Types of Videos and Prompts

### 1. NOVA Consulting Videos
- **Prompt:** "Discuss recent industry trends and how NOVA is positioning itself as a leader."
- **Tone:** Professional and authoritative.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Use infographics and charts to illustrate trends.

### 2. RideWire Nexus Product Demos
- **Prompt:** "Show case the key features of the RideWire Nexus product."
- **Tone:** Informative and engaging.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Screen recordings of the product in action, with annotations.

### 3. RideWire Pro AR Diagnostic Showcases
- **Prompt:** "Demonstrate how RideWire Pro AR enhances diagnostic accuracy."
- **Tone:** Technical and motivational.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Side-by-side comparisons of diagnostic results.

### 4. Customer Testimonials
- **Prompt:** "Gather stories from customers about their positive experiences."
- **Tone:** Warm and optimistic.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Footage of customers in their environments.

### 5. Explainer Videos
- **Prompt:** "Explain how RideWire's solutions can benefit users."
- **Tone:** Educational and persuasive.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Animated graphics to illustrate key points.

### 6. Social Media Shorts
- **Prompt:** "Create quick tips or highlights to engage audiences on social media."
- **Tone:** Casual and catchy.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Fast-paced editing with on-screen text highlights.

### 7. Pitch Deck Videos
- **Prompt:** "Summarize the pitch deck content in an engaging video format."
- **Tone:** Confident and compelling.
- **Visual Suggestions:** Use slides with voiceover to articulate the message.

## Best Practices for Script Structure
- **Hook:** Start with a captivating introduction.
- **Body:** Clearly outline the main points, supported by visuals.
- **Conclusion:** End with a strong call-to-action.

## Visual Suggestions
- Use high-resolution images and graphics.
- Maintain a consistent color scheme and branding.
- Incorporate animations to keep viewers engaged.

## Tone Guidance
- Match the tone to the target audience.
- Use conversational language for social media and more formal language for corporate videos.

## Platform-Specific Optimization
### YouTube
- Use longer, more in-depth content. Optimize thumbnails and descriptions for search.

### LinkedIn
- Focus on professional content, use industry jargon where applicable.

### Twitter
- Keep videos short and engaging; use captions for mute views.

### TikTok
- Create vertical videos with dynamic edits for quick engagement. 

## Conclusion
By following these prompts and best practices, you can create compelling marketing videos that resonate with your audience and effectively showcase RideWire's offerings.
