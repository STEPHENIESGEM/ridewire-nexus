# Video Presentation Recommended Order

## For Initial Meeting (Today)

### Option A: Quick Overview (10 minutes)
1. **Platform Overview** (3 min) - Set context
2. **Multi-AI Demo** (5 min) - Show unique value
3. **Live Demo** (2 min) - Real-time interaction

### Option B: Comprehensive (20 minutes)
1. **Platform Overview** (3 min)
2. **Multi-AI Demo** (5 min)
3. **AR Tool Demo** (4 min)
4. **Workflow Integration** (6 min)
5. **Q&A** (2 min)

## For Follow-Up Presentations

### For Technicians
- Focus: Multi-AI Demo + AR Tool + Workflow
- Skip: Business case, ROI discussion
- Emphasize: Practical benefits, ease of use

### For Management
- Focus: ROI, efficiency gains, customer satisfaction
- Include: Success stories, metrics, business case
- Emphasize: Profitability and competitive advantage

### For Corporate
- Focus: Strategic value, market opportunity, partnership benefits
- Include: All videos
- Emphasize: Technology leadership, network impact

## Presentation Tips
- Always start with brief context (Platform Overview)
- Adjust based on audience engagement
- Be prepared to skip videos if running short on time
- Live demo is more powerful than video when possible
- End with clear call to action

## Backup Plan
If technical issues prevent live demo:
1. Show pre-recorded demos
2. Use screenshots and walk through features
3. Focus on concept and vision vs. live functionality
4. Schedule follow-up for live demonstration
