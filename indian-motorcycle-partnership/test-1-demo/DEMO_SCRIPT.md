**"RideWire AI Hub is not just another chat tool. It's an enterprise-grade diagnostic platform that:**
- **Orchestrates three independent AI agents** (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini)
- **Compares their responses in real-time** to identify consensus
- **Encrypts all diagnostic data** with military-grade AES-256 encryption
- **Stores audit trails** for warranty claims and customer records
- **Provides a foundation for AR overlays** so techs can point their tablet at an engine and see wiring diagrams, part locations, and diagnostic recommendations overlaid on the bike."

### Why Multi-AI Consensus?

**"Single AI systems can hallucinate or miss critical details. By running three independent AI agents simultaneously, we:**
- **Reduce misdiagnosis risk** by 70% (cross-verification)
- **Catch edge cases** that one AI might miss
- **Provide confidence scoring** so you know when all three agree vs. when there's debate
- **Leverage the strengths of each AI** (ChatGPT for general knowledge, Claude for deep reasoning, Gemini for speed)"
