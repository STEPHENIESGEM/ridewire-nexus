# Safety Protocols - RideWire Testing

**Priority**: Safety is #1 in all testing activities

---

## Shop Safety Standards

### Equipment Safety
- All testing devices properly grounded
- No interference with shop safety systems
- Tablet charging only in designated safe areas
- Keep devices away from fuel, chemicals, flammables
- Secure all cables to prevent trip hazards
- Follow standard shop electrical safety protocols

### Motorcycle Testing Safety
- Follow ALL standard shop safety procedures
- Engine running tests: proper ventilation required
- AR testing: maintain awareness of surroundings
- Never use devices while motorcycle is being moved
- Hot component testing: appropriate PPE required
- Lift safety: no device usage near lifts in motion

### Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)
- Standard shop PPE requirements apply
- Safety glasses when working on motorcycles
- Gloves when handling chemicals or hot components
- Hearing protection in high-noise areas
- Steel-toe boots as per shop policy

---

## Data and Network Security

### Network Security
- Secure WiFi connection (WPA3 encrypted) only
- All devices password protected and encrypted
- No public WiFi usage for platform access
- VPN usage when required
- Regular security updates applied

### Data Protection
- Customer data protection at all times
- No customer PII on personal devices
- Secure disposal of printed materials
- Screen privacy in customer-visible areas
- Immediate reporting of security concerns

---

## Emergency Procedures

### Technical Emergencies
- **RideWire Support**: 24/7 emergency hotline
- **Platform Issues**: Immediate escalation protocol
- **Data Concerns**: Security team contact
- **System Failures**: Fallback procedures documented

### Shop Safety Emergencies
- Follow shop emergency procedures
- Know location of fire extinguishers
- Know emergency exits
- Know first aid kit locations
- Emergency contact numbers posted

---

## Compliance Requirements

### Regulatory Compliance
- OSHA shop safety standards maintained
- Fire code compliance
- EPA chemical handling protocols
- ADA accessibility considerations
- Local safety regulations

### Insurance and Liability
- Workers compensation coverage verified
- Liability insurance current
- Equipment insurance documented
- Testing activities covered under shop policy

---

## Training Safety Requirements

### Mandatory Safety Training
- Shop safety orientation completed
- Platform safety features training
- Emergency contact information memorized
- Device handling best practices
- Incident reporting procedures understood

### Ongoing Safety
- Weekly safety reminders
- Monthly safety audits
- Continuous improvement feedback
- Safety suggestion program
- Incident review and learning

---

## Risk Mitigation

### Identified Risks
- **Device damage**: Keep away from hazardous areas
- **Data breach**: Follow security protocols strictly
- **Trip hazards**: Cable management
- **Distraction**: Use platform at appropriate times only
- **Customer privacy**: Screen positioning

### Mitigation Strategies
- Clear usage guidelines
- Regular safety reviews
- Supervised initial usage
- Progressive independence
- Feedback and adjustment
