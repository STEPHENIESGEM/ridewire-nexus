### Part 2: Live Platform Demo (15 minutes)

#### Scenario 1: Check Engine Light - Thunderstroke 111

**Setup**: "Let's say a customer brings in their 2020 Indian Chief with a check engine light on."

**Steps**:
1. **Show login screen**
   - "Service technician logs in securely"
   - "All data is encrypted end-to-end"

2. **Enter diagnostic code**
   - Type: "P0300 - Random Misfire Detected"
   - Show vehicle info: 2020 Indian Chief, Thunderstroke 111

3. **AI Analysis**
   - "Watch as three AI agents analyze simultaneously"
   - Show ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini working

4. **Consensus Result**
   - Display consensus: "Most likely cause: Ignition coil failure on rear cylinder"
   - Show confidence rating: 92%
   - Display recommended tests:
     * Check spark plug resistance
     * Test ignition coil output
     * Inspect wiring harness

5. **Historical Data**
   - "The system also shows this is a known issue for 2018-2021 Thunderstroke engines"
   - Display TSB reference
   - Show repair success rate: 94%

**Talking Points**:
- "Notice how fast that was - about 30 seconds"
- "Traditional diagnostic would take 15-20 minutes of manual research"
- "All three AI agents agreed, giving us high confidence"

#### Scenario 2: Electrical Issue - PowerPlus Engine

**Setup**: "Now let's look at a newer model with intermittent electrical issues."

**Steps**:
1. **Enter symptoms** (not just a code)
   - "2022 Indian FTR 1200"
   - "Intermittent instrument cluster failure"
   - "No stored codes"

2. **AI Diagnostic Interview**
   - System asks follow-up questions:
     * "Does it happen when engine is cold or hot?"
     * "Any correlation with weather conditions?"
     * "Have you noticed any other electrical issues?"

3. **Multi-AI Analysis**
   - Show different AI perspectives:
     * ChatGPT: "Check battery connections"
     * Claude: "Likely CAN bus communication issue"
     * Gemini: "Test ECM ground connections"

4. **Consensus & Action Plan**
   - Weighted consensus: "Check ECM ground and CAN bus"
   - Step-by-step diagnostic procedure
   - Expected time: 20 minutes
   - Parts needed: None (likely just cleaning)

**Talking Points**:
- "Even without a code, the AI can help narrow down the issue"
- "This saves hours of guesswork"
- "The multi-agent approach catches things a single AI might miss"

#### Scenario 3: Performance Issue - Custom Modifications

**Setup**: "Here's a challenging one - modified bike with performance issues."

**Steps**:

   - "2019 Indian Scout Bobber"

2. **AI Analysis with Modifications**

3. **Diagnosis**

**Talking Points**:
- "The AI understands aftermarket modifications"
- "It pulls data from thousands of similar builds"
- "Saves dealership from guessing on modified bikes"
