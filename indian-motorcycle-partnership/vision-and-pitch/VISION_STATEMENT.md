## The Customer Experience We're Building

### Before RideWire

**Customer brings Indian Scout to dealer:**
1. Describes problem to service advisor
2. Waits while tech diagnoses (30-60 minutes)
3. Tech researches error codes, checks forums, calls colleagues
4. Gets repair estimate (sometimes uncertain)
5. Waits for repair (if parts available)
6. Hopes diagnosis was correct

- Long diagnostic wait times
