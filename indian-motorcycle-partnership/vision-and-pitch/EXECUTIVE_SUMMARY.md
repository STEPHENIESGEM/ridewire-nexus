## The Opportunity

### Market Problem
- **Diagnostic Complexity:** Modern Indian motorcycles have sophisticated electronics requiring expert knowledge
- **Technician Training:** New techs need years to master Indian diagnostic procedures
- **Misdiagnosis Costs:** Incorrect diagnostics lead to comebacks, warranty issues, and customer dissatisfaction
- **Time Pressure:** Shops need faster turnaround without sacrificing accuracy
- **Knowledge Gaps:** Service manuals don't always cover edge cases and intermittent issues

### RideWire Solution
A **multi-AI diagnostic platform** that:
- Orchestrates ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini simultaneously for consensus-based diagnostics
- Provides **70% faster diagnostic times** compared to traditional methods
- Delivers **95% diagnostic accuracy** through AI cross-verification
- Offers **AR overlays** for visual guidance on Indian motorcycles
- Creates **encrypted audit trails** for warranty and compliance
