## Problem Statement

### Current Pain Points in Motorcycle Diagnostics

**For Dealers:**
1. **Diagnostic Time Waste** - Techs spend 30-60 minutes per diagnostic
2. **Misdiagnosis Risk** - 15-20% of initial diagnostics are incorrect
3. **Comeback Costs** - Repeat repairs cost dealers $500-$1,500 each
4. **Training Challenges** - New techs need 2-3 years to become proficient
5. **Knowledge Gaps** - Service manuals don't cover all edge cases
6. **Revenue Leakage** - Slow diagnostics = fewer customers served

**For Customers:**
1. **Long Wait Times** - Drop off bike for days just for diagnosis
2. **Uncertain Accuracy** - "Hope they got it right"
3. **Lack of Transparency** - Don't understand what's wrong
4. **Higher Costs** - Pay for misdiagnosed repairs
5. **Frustration** - Multiple visits for same issue

**For Indian Motorcycle Corporate:**
1. **Inconsistent Service Quality** - Varies widely across dealers
2. **Warranty Costs** - Misdiagnoses drive up warranty claims
3. **Customer Satisfaction** - Service issues hurt brand reputation
4. **Competitive Pressure** - Harley, BMW investing in digital tools
5. **Data Blind Spots** - Don't know what's failing until warranty claims

## Competitive Landscape

### Traditional Diagnostic Tools

**Mitchell1 / AllData:**
- Static service manuals and wiring diagrams
- No AI or real-time analysis
- One-time purchase or annual subscription
- Pricing: $1,000-$3,000/year per shop

**Snap-on / Mac Tools:**
- Hardware diagnostic scanners
- Expensive ($3,000-$10,000)
- No AI assistance
- Focused on reading codes, not interpretation

**OEM Diagnostic Tools:**
- Indian-specific diagnostic software (exists but limited)
- Basic code reading
- No AR, no AI interpretation
- Included with dealer authorization

### Emerging AI Solutions

**Generic ChatGPT / Claude:**
- Single AI (no consensus)
- Not motorcycle-specific
- No integration with shop workflows
- Risk of "hallucinations" (incorrect responses)

**Automotive AI Platforms:**
- Focused on cars, not motorcycles
- No AR overlays
- Not tailored to Indian Motorcycle
- Generic recommendations
