# Brand Usage Proposal - Indian Motorcycle Partnership
## How RideWire Will Use the Indian Motorcycle Name and Brand

---

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT LEGAL FRAMEWORK

**All usage of Indian Motorcycle trademarks, logos, and brand assets is subject to:**
- Written approval from Indian Motorcycle corporate
- Compliance with Indian Motorcycle brand guidelines
- Annual review and renewal of licensing agreement
- Immediate cessation upon termination of partnership

**RideWire acknowledges that Indian Motorcycle owns all rights to:**
- Indian Motorcycle® name and trademarks
- Indian Motorcycle logos and visual identity
- Model names (Scout®, Chief®, Challenger®, FTR®, Springfield®)
- All related intellectual property

---

## Proposed Brand Usage

### 1. Product Naming

#### Primary Product Name
**"RideWire for Indian Motorcycle"**
- Clear distinction: RideWire is the platform, Indian Motorcycle is the partner
- Not "Indian Motorcycle Diagnostics" (too official)
- Not "Indian AI Tool" (implies Indian owns/developed it)

#### Alternative Names (Subject to Approval)
- "RideWire AI Hub - Indian Motorcycle Edition"
- "RideWire Diagnostic Platform - Authorized for Indian Motorcycle"
- "RideWire Pro - Indian Motorcycle Dealer Network"

#### What We Will NOT Use Without Explicit Permission
- ❌ "Indian Motorcycle's Official Diagnostic Tool"
- ❌ "Indian Diagnostic AI"
- ❌ Any name implying Indian developed or endorses as official product
- ❌ Standalone use of "Indian" without "Motorcycle" and ® symbol

---

### 2. Logo and Visual Identity Usage

#### Permitted Uses (Subject to Approval)

**A. Co-Branding**
```
[RideWire Logo] + [for] + [Indian Motorcycle Logo]
```
- RideWire logo on left
- "for" or "Authorized for" in plain text
- Indian Motorcycle logo on right (per brand guidelines)
- Clear visual separation between brands

**B. Dealer Materials**
- Indian Motorcycle logo in corner of marketing materials
- "Authorized Tool for Indian Motorcycle Dealers" badge
- Co-branded business cards for sales team
- Joint press release letterhead

**C. Digital Properties**
- Indian Motorcycle logo on RideWire dealer dashboard (small, unobtrusive)
- Partner page on ridewire.ai website
- Social media posts (with proper attribution)

#### Prohibited Uses
- ❌ Modifying Indian Motorcycle logo in any way
- ❌ Using Indian logo larger than RideWire logo
- ❌ Implying endorsement beyond what's contractually agreed
- ❌ Using Indian brand assets for non-Indian-Motorcycle products
- ❌ Using outdated or unofficial Indian logos

### 8. Competitive and Exclusivity Considerations

#### During Exclusivity Period (2 Years Proposed)

**RideWire Agrees:**
- Will not partner with direct Indian Motorcycle competitors (Harley-Davidson during exclusivity)
- Will not market to independent shops as "Indian Motorcycle tool"
- Will prioritize Indian Motorcycle feature development
- Will give Indian first look at new features

**Indian Motorcycle Agrees:**
- Will not endorse competing diagnostic AI platforms
- Will give RideWire preferred status in dealer communications
- Will collaborate on co-marketing opportunities
- Will provide early access to new model technical data

#### After Exclusivity Period
- RideWire may partner with other OEMs
- Indian retains co-branding rights if partnership continues
- RideWire can continue using "Authorized for Indian Motorcycle" 
- Revenue sharing continues at agreed rates

#### Required Disclaimers on All Materials

**Short Form (Minimum):**
```
RideWire is a third-party diagnostic assistance tool. It is not owned, 
operated, or officially endorsed by Indian Motorcycle. All diagnostics 
must be verified by certified Indian Motorcycle technicians.
```

**Long Form (Detailed Materials):**
```
RideWire AI Hub is an independent diagnostic assistance platform that 
partners with Indian Motorcycle dealerships. RideWire is not manufactured, 
endorsed, or warranted by Indian Motorcycle. The Indian Motorcycle name, 
logos, and model names are trademarks of Indian Motorcycle International, LLC 
and are used under license. All AI-generated diagnostics are for informational 
purposes only and must be verified by certified mechanics before repairs are 
performed. RideWire does not replace professional automotive repair services.
```

**Placement:**
- Footer of all marketing materials
- About page of RideWire software
- Terms of service
- Dealer agreements
- Customer invoices (if RideWire mentioned)
