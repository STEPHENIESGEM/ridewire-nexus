# Frequently Asked Questions

## About RideWire AI Hub

**Q: What is RideWire AI Hub?**
A: A multi-AI diagnostic platform that combines ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini with AR technology for vehicle diagnostics.

**Q: How is this different from other diagnostic tools?**
A: We're the only platform using three AI agents that collaborate and cross-validate. Plus AR overlays for visual diagnostics.

**Q: Does it work on motorcycles only?**
A: Currently optimized for motorcycles (Indian Motorcycle specifically), but technology works on any vehicle type.

## Technical Questions

**Q: What devices do I need?**

**Q: Do I need internet connection?**

**Q: How fast is it?**
A: AI consensus typically returns in under 5 seconds. AR overlays are real-time.

**Q: What if one AI is wrong?**
