# Brand Licensing Terms

## RideWire Use of Indian Motorcycle Brand
- "Tested at Indian Motorcycle" certification (with approval)
- Case studies and testimonials (with permission)
- Logo usage per brand guidelines
- Partnership announcements (mutual approval)

## Indian Motorcycle Use of RideWire Brand
- "Powered by RideWire AI Hub" in dealer materials
- Technology reference in marketing
- Co-branded training materials

## Restrictions
- No product naming using other's trademarks
- No endorsement claims without agreement
- Cease usage upon partnership termination

## Approval Process
- Tier 1: Pre-approved uses
- Tier 2: 5-day standard approval
- Tier 3: 10-15 day executive approval
