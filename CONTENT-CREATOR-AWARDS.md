## üó≥Ô∏è Voting System: "FANS VOTE"

### Fan Voting Process:

**Phase 1: Nominations (2 months before ceremony)**
- AI automatically nominates top performers by category
- Community can nominate favorite creators
- Nominees contacted, profiles featured on platform

**Phase 2: Voting (1 month voting period)**
- All RideWire users can vote (free + paid)
- 1 vote per category per user
- Weighted system:
  - Community votes: 50%
  - AI quality scoring: 25%
  - Platform metrics (views, engagement): 25%

**Phase 3: Live Ceremony (Annual event)**
- Virtual ceremony streamed on YouTube/Twitch
- Winners announced live
- Cash prizes distributed immediately
- Vacation packages booked on-the-spot

### Voting Categories:

Users vote on:
1. **Best Overall Video** (grand prize)
2. **Best Diagnostic Video**
3. **Best Tutorial Series**
4. **Most Helpful Expert** (chat)
5. **Rising Star Expert**
6. **Best Troubleshooter**
7. **6 Category Awards**
8. **People's Choice** (any video, pure fan vote)

**Video Awards:**
