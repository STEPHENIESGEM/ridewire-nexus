## Best Practices

### Before Sending:
1. ‚úÖ Watch their 3 most recent videos
2. ‚úÖ Note specific topics/quotes you can reference
3. ‚úÖ Check their bio for business email (don't use generic contact forms)
4. ‚úÖ Verify subscriber count and engagement rate
5. ‚úÖ Customize subject line to match their content style

### During Outreach:
1. ‚úÖ Send between 9am-11am or 2pm-4pm (their time zone)
2. ‚úÖ Use plain text emails (no heavy HTML formatting)
3. ‚úÖ Keep emails under 200 words
4. ‚úÖ Include clear call-to-action
5. ‚úÖ Make unsubscribing easy ("No worries if not interested")

### After Sending:
1. ‚úÖ Log in database: Date_Contacted, Status = "Sent"
2. ‚úÖ Set reminder for Day 5 follow-up
3. ‚úÖ Track opens/clicks if using email tracking tool
4. ‚úÖ Respond within 2 hours if they reply
5. ‚úÖ Update Partnership_Status in database
