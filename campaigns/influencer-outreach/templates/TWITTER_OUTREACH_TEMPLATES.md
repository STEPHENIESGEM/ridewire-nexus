## Overview
Twitter/X outreach is unique because:
1. **Public engagement first**: Like, retweet, quote-tweet before DMing
2. **Thread culture**: Influencers love detailed threads
3. **DMs are limited**: You can't DM unless they follow you or have open DMs
4. **Email is primary**: Most outreach happens via email listed in bio

## Twitter-Specific Best Practices

### Public Engagement First:
1. ‚úÖ Like 10-15 recent tweets before reaching out
2. ‚úÖ Retweet 2-3 of their best tweets
3. ‚úÖ Leave thoughtful replies (not generic "Great post!")
4. ‚úÖ Quote-tweet with added value
5. ‚úÖ Tag them when relevant (don't spam)

### DM Guidelines:
1. ‚úÖ Only DM if they follow you or have open DMs
2. ‚úÖ Keep messages under 280 characters
3. ‚úÖ Be professional but casual (match Twitter tone)
4. ‚úÖ Include clear call-to-action
5. ‚úÖ Don't spam multiple DMs

### Email Guidelines:
1. ‚úÖ Reference specific tweets/threads (shows you did research)
2. ‚úÖ Use their Twitter handle in subject line
3. ‚úÖ Keep it concise (Twitter users scan quickly)
4. ‚úÖ Include links to demo/resources
5. ‚úÖ Make commission structure crystal clear
