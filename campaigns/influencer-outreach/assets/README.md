## Brand Guidelines

### Logo Usage
- **Primary Logo**: Full color (use on white backgrounds)
- **Secondary Logo**: White version (use on dark backgrounds)
- **Minimum Size**: 100px wide (digital), 1 inch wide (print)
- **Clear Space**: Leave at least 0.5x logo height of clear space around logo

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue**: #0066FF
- **Secondary Dark**: #1A1A1A
- **Accent Green**: #00CC66
- **Background Light**: #F5F5F5
- **Text Dark**: #333333

### Typography
- **Headings**: Inter Bold or Helvetica Neue Bold
- **Body Text**: Inter Regular or Arial
- **Code/Technical**: Fira Code or Courier New

### Tone of Voice
- **Professional but approachable**: Not corporate, but not too casual
- **Technical when needed**: Use technical terms with explanations
- **Transparent**: Honest about AI limitations
- **Empowering**: Focus on user benefits
