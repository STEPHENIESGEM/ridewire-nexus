## Red Flags & Troubleshooting

### Red Flag: Low Response Rate (<10%)
**Actions**:
- [ ] Review and revise email templates
- [ ] Test new subject lines
- [ ] Try different outreach times
- [ ] Switch to LinkedIn or Instagram DMs

### Red Flag: High Decline Rate (>50% of responders decline)
**Actions**:
- [ ] Review commission structure (too low?)
- [ ] Improve demo materials
- [ ] Clarify value proposition
- [ ] Offer better incentives

### Red Flag: Partners Not Posting Content
**Actions**:
- [ ] Provide content ideas and templates
- [ ] Offer higher commission as incentive
- [ ] Schedule check-in calls
- [ ] Make it easier (pre-made graphics, scripts)

### Red Flag: Low Conversion from Content
**Actions**:
- [ ] Review affiliate links (working?)
- [ ] Improve landing page
- [ ] Better influencer CTAs
- [ ] Optimize pricing or offer

## Time Management Tips

### Batch Work
- **Emails**: Write 50 at once, schedule to send throughout day
- **Research**: Dedicate full mornings to research phases
- **Responses**: Set specific times (10am, 2pm, 5pm) to check inbox

### Use Templates
- Save email responses as Gmail canned responses
- Use text expander for common phrases
- Automate what you can (Zapier is your friend)

### Prioritize High-Value Activities
1. **Highest Priority**: Respond to active partners
2. **High Priority**: Hot leads (replied positively)
3. **Medium Priority**: Follow-ups (Day 5, 10)
4. **Low Priority**: New outreach (can be automated)
