## THE "MOVIE MODE" FRAMEWORK

### What is Movie Mode?
Every great investor pitch starts with a scene, not a statistic. The reader should feel transported into the moment where the problem is real, the stakes are high, and the solution is obvious.

**Structure:**
1. **SCENE** (1-2 sentences): Vivid, specific, emotional moment
2. **PROBLEM** (2-3 sentences): Why this matters, scale of pain
3. **SOLUTION** (2-3 sentences): What we built, how it works
4. **PROOF** (2-3 sentences): Customer validation, metrics, traction
5. **ASK** (1-2 sentences): Clear next step, respect their time

**Tone:** Confident, specific, founder-to-founder. Never salesy. Always backed by data.

**The Moat:** "Human-AI Family" weaves through every script as the central differentiator. This isn't a product feature. This is our philosophy, our architecture, our brand promise. And it resonates because it's real.
