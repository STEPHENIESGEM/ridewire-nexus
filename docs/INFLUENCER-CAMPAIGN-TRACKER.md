## V. PARTNERSHIP DEAL STRUCTURES

### Option 1: Flat Fee
- One-time payment for dedicated content
- Pricing tiers:
  - Tier 1 (100K+ followers): $5,000-$10,000
  - Tier 2 (25K-100K followers): $1,000-$5,000
  - Tier 3 (5K-25K followers): $250-$1,000

### Option 2: Revenue Share (Affiliate)
- Commission on all referred users who convert to paid plans
- Tiers:
  - Exclusive partner: 30-50% lifetime commission
  - Standard partner: 20-30% first-year commission
  - Micro-influencer: 15-25% first-month commission

### Option 3: Hybrid (Flat Fee + Revenue Share)
- Upfront payment + ongoing commission
- Example: $2,000 flat + 20% commission for 6 months

### Option 4: Equity Partnership (Top-Tier Only)
- Reserved for mega-influencers (1M+ followers)
- Small equity stake (0.1-0.5%) in exchange for long-term endorsement

## VI. CAMPAIGN PERFORMANCE TRACKING

### Key Metrics to Monitor

**Traffic Metrics:**
- Impressions (total reach of influencer content)
- Clicks (UTM-tracked link clicks)
- Click-through rate (CTR)

**Conversion Metrics:**
- Sign-ups (new user registrations from influencer)
- Activation rate (% of sign-ups who complete first diagnostic)
- Paid conversion rate (% of sign-ups who upgrade to paid plan)

**Revenue Metrics:**
- Revenue generated (total $ from influencer referrals)
- Average revenue per user (ARPU from influencer cohort)
- Customer lifetime value (LTV from influencer cohort)

**ROI Metrics:**
- Cost per acquisition (CPA = Campaign cost / Sign-ups)
- Return on ad spend (ROAS = Revenue / Campaign cost)
- Net profit (Revenue - Campaign cost - Commission payouts)

## VII. AUTOMATION WORKFLOWS (HubSpot/Zapier)

### Workflow 1: Auto Follow-Up Sequence
- Trigger: Email sent to influencer
- Day 3: Auto-send follow-up #1 if no response
- Day 7: Auto-send follow-up #2 if no response
- Day 14: Auto-send final follow-up if no response
- Day 15: Mark as "No Response" and remove from active pipeline

### Workflow 2: Partner Onboarding
- Trigger: Influencer agrees to partnership
- Action 1: Send partnership agreement for e-signature
- Action 2: Create Pro account and send login credentials
- Action 3: Send influencer asset pack (logo files, demo video, key talking points)
- Action 4: Generate unique UTM tracking link
- Action 5: Schedule check-in call 1 week before content launch

### Workflow 3: Performance Alerts
- Trigger: Campaign goes live (content published)
- Action 1: Daily email with performance snapshot (clicks, sign-ups, revenue)
- Action 2: Alert if campaign underperforms (<50 clicks in first 48 hours)
- Action 3: Alert if campaign overperforms (>500 clicks in first 48 hours)

### Workflow 4: Commission Payouts
- Trigger: Monthly billing cycle ends
- Action 1: Calculate commission owed to each influencer
- Action 2: Send payout summary email
- Action 3: Process payment via Stripe/PayPal
- Action 4: Send payment confirmation receipt
