## Tone Guidelines

### Professional & Friendly
- Use conversational but professional language
- Be warm and approachable
- Avoid jargon unless technical audience

### Clear & Concise
- Get to the point quickly
- Use bullet points for clarity
- Break up long paragraphs

### Helpful & Proactive
- Anticipate follow-up questions
- Provide relevant links and resources
- Offer next steps

### Legally Compliant
- Always include disclaimer for diagnostic advice
- Remind users to consult professionals
- Never guarantee repair outcomes
