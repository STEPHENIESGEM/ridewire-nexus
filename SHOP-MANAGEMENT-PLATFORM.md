## üõ°Ô∏è Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Incumbent Competition
**Threat:** Mitchell 1, Shop-Ware fight back with price cuts or feature matching
**Mitigation:** 
- Build AI moat (they can't match quickly)
- Focus on mobile mechanics first (they ignore)
- Lock in customers with integrations
- Move fast, iterate weekly

### Risk 2: Slow Adoption (Shops Resist Change)
**Threat:** Shops comfortable with existing tools, hesitant to switch
**Mitigation:**
- Free data migration from competitors
- White-glove onboarding for first 1,000 customers
- "Try before you buy" 30-day trial
- Success stories & case studies

### Risk 3: Technical Complexity
**Threat:** Building 8 modules is much more complex than diagnostics alone
**Mitigation:**
- Phase approach (MVP ‚Üí iterative expansion)
- Hire experienced SaaS engineers
- Use proven tech stack (React, Node, PostgreSQL)
- Partner integrations where possible (don't reinvent)

### Risk 4: Customer Support Burden
**Threat:** 5,000 shops = 5,000 different workflows, many support requests
**Mitigation:**
- Extensive documentation & video tutorials
- In-app help & tooltips
- AI chatbot for common questions
- Tiered support (enterprise gets priority)
- Community forum (users help each other)

### Risk 5: Payment Processing Risk
**Threat:** Holding customer money (invoices) creates liability, regulatory burden
**Mitigation:**
- Use Stripe Connect (they handle compliance)
- Don't hold funds (instant payout to shop bank accounts)
- Insurance for payment processor errors
- Clear terms of service
