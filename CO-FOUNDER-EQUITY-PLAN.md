## Vesting Schedule

**All equity vests over 4 years with a 1-year cliff:**

- **Year 1 (Cliff):** 25% vests on the 1-year anniversary
  - If someone leaves before 1 year, they get 0% (shares return to company)
  - Protects the company from early departures

- **Years 2-4:** Remaining 75% vests monthly (2.08% per month)
  - After Year 1, you earn ~2% of your total shares each month
  - Fully vested after 4 years

### Example (Tracy's 30%):
- **Month 12:** 7.5% vested (25% of 30%)
- **Month 18:** 10.5% vested (7.5% + 6 months Ã— 0.625%)
- **Month 24:** 15% vested (halfway done)
- **Month 48:** 30% fully vested

**Why vesting?**
- Industry standard (protects against early departures)
- Fair to everyone (earn your shares over time)
- Investors require it (can't raise funding without vesting)
